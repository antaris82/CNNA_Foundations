# Notes

## Epistemic status

Numerical diagnostic only. No physical `i`, no complex Hilbert space, no C*-norm, no GNS representation, and no AQFT net are derived.

## What is new relative to the previous test

The previous Record/Live block adjoint test checked whether immediate reverse handoff generators contain the DtN adjoints. They did not.

This test asks whether the missing adjoint directions become visible after generating a finite real operator system from:

- block longitudinal operators `L_a`, `L_b`,
- transverse handoffs `T_ab^k`, `T_ba^k`,
- projectors onto the two local Record/Live spaces,
- products up to degree 3.

## Current bottleneck

The basis-adjoint and one-more-product residuals remain too large in the real-growth L5 sample. The next methodologically clean step is not `J` extraction, but generator enrichment: add derived history-intertwiner/parallel-transport candidates between local mini-planes, or derive a richer boundary-history space.
